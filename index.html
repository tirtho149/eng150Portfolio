About Me

My name is Tirtho Roy, and I am currently a sophomore majoring in Computer Science at Iowa State University. Throughout my academic journey, I've been developing skills as a strategic thinker and communicator, able to traverse across genres and media.

When I began ENGL 1500, I set several goals for myself: to be an effective communicator of personal stories, to render expert interviews into forms accessible to different media, and to present an academic argument clearly and precisely.

I now see communication not as a task but as a responsibility. Whether I am honoring the memory of a loved one, informing the public about policy, or working with a partner in another time zone, I am committed to clarity, compassion, and purpose.
